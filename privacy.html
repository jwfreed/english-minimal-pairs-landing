Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 26, 2025

Introduction

Minimal Pairs Trainer ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use the core features of our app. Your practice data and progress are stored locally on your device.

Information Collected Automatically

We may collect certain information automatically when you use our app, including:

  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app, such as features used and time spent
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version
  • Analytics Data: We use analytics services to understand how our app is used and to improve our services

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our app
  • Understand how users interact with our app
  • Develop new features and services
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Communicate with you about updates and changes

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. These services include:

  • App Store & Google Play: Purchase and download information
  • Analytics Services: For app usage analytics and crash reporting

These third-party services have their own privacy policies addressing how they use such information.

Data Storage and Security

Your practice progress and settings are stored locally on your device. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
privacy@minimalpairstrainer.com
